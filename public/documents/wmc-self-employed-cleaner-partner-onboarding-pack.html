Cleaner Partner Details

Status notice: WMC is a cleaning marketplace, not an employer. Cleaner partners are independent self-employed providers. They are responsible for their own tax, insurance, equipment, travel, availability, service delivery and legal compliance.

Section 1 - Cleaner Partner Agreement

1. Independent self-employed status

The cleaner confirms they are applying to join West Midlands Cleaner (WMC) as an independent self-employed cleaner partner. The cleaner is not an employee, worker, agent, franchisee or representative of WMC. The cleaner is responsible for their own tax position, National Insurance, expenses, tools, equipment, insurance, travel, records and legal compliance. As a self-employed cleaner partner, the cleaner is not entitled to National Minimum Wage, holiday pay, sick pay, pension contributions or other employment-related benefits from WMC.

2. WMC marketplace role

WMC operates as a cleaning marketplace. Customers post cleaning requests and approved providers may review suitable job details and submit quotes. WMC does not guarantee work, bookings, enquiries, shifts, wages, income or minimum hours. The cleaner may choose whether to quote for suitable opportunities made available through the platform.

3. Quoting and acceptance

Submitting a quote or showing interest does not guarantee the booking. Customers may compare available quotes and choose another provider. The cleaner must only quote for work they are willing and able to complete and must check service type, location, date, access notes, parking notes, products, expected time and customer requirements before quoting.

4. Right of substitution

The cleaner may propose a suitable substitute to complete a booking, but no substitute may attend a customer property unless they have completed WMC's required checks, insurance requirements, right-to-work or eligibility checks where applicable, and platform approval before the booking. The cleaner remains responsible for ensuring any proposed substitute is suitable and that the customer booking can be completed safely and professionally.

5. Service delivery responsibility

The cleaner is responsible for carrying out accepted work professionally, safely and to a reasonable standard. The cleaner is responsible for suitable products, equipment and travel unless the confirmed booking details say otherwise. The cleaner must not attend a customer property unless WMC confirms that the booking is ready to proceed and the full job details have been released.

6. No bypassing WMC

Customers introduced through WMC remain WMC-introduced customers. The cleaner must not request direct payment outside WMC, ask for cash side-payments, encourage a customer to avoid WMC, or use WMC customer details to take private work away from the platform.

7. Pausing, suspension or removal

WMC may pause, suspend or remove access to platform opportunities where there are concerns about safety, trust, insurance, eligibility, customer data, conduct, complaints, no-shows, bypassing WMC, unpaid fees, disputes or serious breach of platform terms.

Section 2 - Compliance Declaration

1. Tax and HMRC responsibility

The cleaner is responsible for checking their own tax position, registering with HMRC where required, keeping accurate records and declaring income. WMC may ask whether the cleaner is already registered with HMRC, intends to register when required, or expects their self-employed trading income to remain within any applicable allowance.

2. Public liability insurance

The cleaner must have suitable public liability insurance before accepting or completing any paid WMC booking. WMC may request evidence of insurance before approval and at any time during platform access. WMC may pause access to opportunities until required evidence is received.

3. Right to work, identity and eligibility

The cleaner confirms they are eligible to work in the UK and can provide evidence if requested. WMC may request right-to-work, identity, eligibility or immigration-status evidence where applicable before any paid booking opportunities are offered.

4. Health and safety

The cleaner agrees to work safely, use products correctly, avoid unsafe chemical mixing, follow relevant instructions, stop work if a property or task appears unsafe and report hazards, accidents, damage, injury or access issues to WMC as soon as possible.

5. Customer information and data protection

The cleaner must use customer names, addresses, phone numbers, photos, access notes and booking details only for the relevant WMC booking. Customer information must not be shared, posted online, reused for private marketing or used to bypass WMC. Access information should be deleted or destroyed once it is no longer needed for the booking. The cleaner may request access, correction or deletion of personal data, subject to WMC's legal, tax, fraud-prevention, insurance, dispute-handling and record-retention obligations.

6. Conduct standards

The cleaner must treat customers, properties, keys, access details and possessions with care and respect. The cleaner must not smoke, vape, drink alcohol, use drugs or bring unauthorised people to a customer property during a WMC booking.

Section 3 - Quote, Payment and Payout Terms

1. Customer payment

If a customer chooses the cleaner's quote, the customer pays WMC securely through the platform before the booking is confirmed. The cleaner must not ask the customer to pay directly outside WMC for a WMC-introduced booking.

2. WMC platform fee

WMC keeps a 15% platform fee from the total gross amount paid by the customer for confirmed, completed bookings. The cleaner payout is the customer payment minus the WMC platform fee and any relevant deductions, refunds, chargebacks, disputes or agreed adjustments.

Customer pays WMC - total grossGBP 100.00
WMC platform fee - 15%GBP 15.00

3. Payout timing and method

Payout is not instant. Payout is normally released after the job is completed and after the 48-hour issue window has passed, provided there is no unresolved customer complaint, refund request, dispute, missing information, chargeback or payment issue. The cleaner is responsible for setting up and maintaining their own Stripe Express account or any payout method made available by WMC. Once WMC releases a payout, Stripe Express pays the provider bank according to the cleaner's Stripe payout schedule.

4. Refunds, issues and disputes

If a customer raises an issue, WMC may pause, reduce, delay or withhold payout while the matter is reviewed. The cleaner agrees to cooperate with WMC, provide information where reasonably requested and respond promptly to issue enquiries.

5. Cancellations, lateness and no-shows

The cleaner must tell WMC immediately if they cannot attend, may be late or accepted a booking by mistake. Repeated late cancellations, no-shows, poor communication or accepting jobs the cleaner cannot attend may result in fewer opportunities, suspension or removal from WMC.

6. Travel, parking and extra costs

Travel costs, parking charges, clean air zone charges and additional expenses are not automatically covered unless WMC confirms this in the booking or the cleaner includes them clearly in the accepted quote.
