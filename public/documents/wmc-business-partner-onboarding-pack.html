Business Partner Onboarding Pack
This document forms part of the onboarding agreement between your cleaning business and West Midlands Cleaner (WMC), alongside any applicable WMC platform terms, policies and booking rules. Please read all three sections carefully. A single signature at the end confirms your agreement to all sections. Submission of this signed form is required for review but does not guarantee approval. WMC reserves the right to approve or decline applications at its sole discretion.
Your Business Details
Section 1 — Business Partner Agreement
1. Independent Business Status

The business confirms that it is applying to join West Midlands Cleaner (WMC) as an independent cleaning business provider. The business understands and agrees that it is not employed by WMC and is not a worker, employee, agent, franchisee or representative of WMC. The business is solely responsible for its own staff, subcontractors, tax, insurance, equipment, transport, training, supervision, service delivery and legal compliance.

2. WMC Marketplace Role

WMC operates as a cleaning marketplace. Customers post cleaning requests, and approved providers may be invited to review job details and submit quotes. WMC does not guarantee that the business will receive work, bookings, quotes, enquiries, income or minimum hours. Acceptance of this signed document by WMC does not guarantee approval to the platform. WMC reserves the right to approve or decline any application at its discretion.

3. Quoting for Jobs

The business agrees to only quote for jobs it is able and willing to complete. The business is responsible for checking job details, location, service type, access notes, parking notes and customer requirements before submitting a quote. Quotes must not be misleading, false or unrealistic.

4. Customer Payment and WMC Platform Fee

Customers pay WMC securely when choosing a provider quote. WMC keeps a 15% platform fee from the total gross amount paid by the customer (including any extra services or surcharges) for confirmed, completed bookings. The provider payout is the total customer payment minus the WMC platform fee and any relevant deductions, refunds, disputes or adjustments.

5. Service Delivery Responsibility

The business is responsible for carrying out the cleaning service professionally and safely, and for providing suitable staff, equipment and products (unless the customer booking details state otherwise). The business is solely responsible and financially liable for any damage caused to a customer's property by its staff or subcontractors during a booking.

6. Insurance and Compliance

The business agrees to maintain suitable public liability insurance before accepting or completing paid jobs through WMC. WMC reserves the right to request a copy of a valid insurance certificate before approving the account, or at any time during the partnership. WMC may pause approval or access to paid opportunities until requested evidence is received. The business is responsible for complying with all applicable UK laws, tax rules, employment regulations, health and safety obligations, data protection obligations and customer protection obligations.

7. Customer Information (Data Protection)

The business must only use customer information for the purpose of quoting for or completing the WMC booking. Customer details must not be misused, shared, sold or used to bypass WMC. The business must not contact WMC customers to avoid the WMC platform fee. The business must securely delete or destroy customer property and access details (such as key safe codes) immediately after the booking is fully completed, in accordance with UK GDPR obligations.

8. Complaints, Issues and Removal

WMC operates a 48-hour issue window after a booking is completed. If a customer raises an issue, complaint, refund request or dispute, WMC may pause payout while the issue is reviewed. WMC may reject, suspend or remove a business from the platform if WMC believes the business has breached platform rules, provided poor service, misused customer data, acted dishonestly, avoided fees, or created risk for customers or WMC.

Section 2 — Compliance Declaration
1. Insurance

The business confirms that it has or will obtain suitable public liability insurance before accepting or completing any paid WMC booking. WMC reserves the right to request a copy of a valid insurance certificate before approving the account, or at any time during the partnership. WMC may pause approval or access to paid opportunities until requested evidence is received.

2. Staff and Right to Work

The business confirms that it is responsible for ensuring that any person it sends to a WMC booking has the legal right to work in the UK where applicable. The business understands that WMC does not employ, supervise or control the business's staff.

3. Tax and Legal Responsibility

The business confirms that it is responsible for its own tax, accounting, VAT position, payroll, staff payments, subcontractor payments, National Insurance, HMRC obligations and business records.

4. Equipment, Health and Safety

The business confirms that it is responsible for providing suitable equipment and products (unless stated otherwise in the booking). The business is responsible for carrying out all work safely and following relevant UK health and safety requirements.

5. Data Protection

The business agrees to use customer information only for the relevant WMC booking. Customer details must not be used for unrelated marketing, resale, bypassing WMC or any unauthorised purpose. The business must securely delete or destroy customer property and access details once a booking is fully completed, in line with UK GDPR.

Section 3 — Quote, Payment and Payout Terms
1. Quote Submission

Submitting a quote does not guarantee the booking. The customer may compare quotes and choose another provider.

2. Customer Payment and No Bypassing

If a customer chooses your quote, the customer pays WMC securely through the platform before the booking is confirmed. The business must not ask the customer to pay directly outside WMC for a WMC booking. The business agrees not to use customer details from WMC to avoid WMC fees, take direct payment for WMC bookings, or move WMC customers away from the platform.

3. WMC Platform Fee

WMC keeps a 15% platform fee from the total gross amount paid by the customer (including any extra services or surcharges) for confirmed, completed bookings.

4. Payout Timing and Method

Payout is not instant. Payout may be released after the job is completed and after the strict 48-hour issue window has passed, provided there is no unresolved customer complaint, refund request, dispute, missing information or payment issue. Payouts for completed jobs will be processed using the payout method made available by WMC, which may include Stripe Connect, bank transfer, or another approved payout method.

5. Refunds, Issues and Disputes

If a customer raises an issue, WMC may pause, reduce or delay payout while the matter is reviewed. The business agrees to cooperate with WMC and provide information where reasonably requested.
